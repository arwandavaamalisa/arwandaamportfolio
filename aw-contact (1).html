Common Questions

Frequently Asked

QHow fast can you deliver a website?

Landing pages are delivered in 1–3 business days. Multi-page websites take 3–7 days. Timeline starts after you send all assets (logo, photos, content).

QDo I need to provide photos and content?

Yes, ideally. Your own photos make the website more authentic. If you don't have photos, I can use high-quality stock photos from Unsplash as placeholders.

QWhat payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer (BCA, Mandiri), GoPay, OVO, QRIS, and PayPal for international clients. 50% DP to start, 50% upon completion.

QWill the website work on mobile?

Absolutely. Every website I build is mobile-first and fully responsive. It'll look great on phones, tablets, and desktops.

QDo you work with international clients?

Yes! I regularly work with clients from Canada, Australia, and beyond. Communication via WhatsApp, email, or video call.

QWhat if I want changes after delivery?

Each package includes revisions (1x for Starter, 2x for Professional). Additional revisions or major changes after delivery are charged at a reasonable rate.
